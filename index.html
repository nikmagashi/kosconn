How we work

1 Initial contact →
2 Discovery & requirements →
3 Analysis & design (ITIL: Plan) →
4 Build & transition (ITIL: Build/Transition) →
5 Operate & support (ITIL: Operate) →
6 Review & improve (CSI)
1. Initial contact

We discuss goals, constraints and timelines. Short, focused call — then you get a one‑page proposal.

2–3. Discovery & Analysis

Workshops and quick audits to understand current state. We define KPIs, risks and success criteria and draft the delivery plan.

4. Build & Transition

Agile sprints with weekly demos. Changes are documented and reviewed. We prepare runbooks and handover materials.

5. Operate & Support

Monitoring, incident response and small enhancements. We keep the lights on while planning the next increment.

6. Continual Improvement

We revisit KPIs and costs quarterly and propose concrete optimizations — new features, refactors or savings.

Working style
  • Single source of truth in your tools (Jira, Git, Teams)
  • Weekly demos; no surprise releases
  • Security, testing and docs by default
